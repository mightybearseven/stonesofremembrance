[YOUR NAME OR COMPANY] ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Stones of Remembrance mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

We have built Stones of Remembrance with privacy as a core principle. Your Stones of Remembrance, journal entries, and personal reflections belong to you.

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit the contents of your journal entries. All entries, titles, tags, and reflections you write are stored locally on your device. We never see this content. We never transmit it to our servers because we do not operate servers that receive it.

We do not collect or use any of the following:

- The contents of your Stones of Remembrance or journal entries
- Photos, audio, or other media you attach to entries
- Your name, email address, or contact information (unless you voluntarily contact us)
- Your precise location
- Your contacts, photo library, or other personal data on your device
- Biometric data
- Browsing or search history
- Information about other apps on your device

## 2. Information We May Collect

The App may collect limited information necessary for its operation:

**Device Information:** Basic, non-identifying technical information such as your device type, iOS version, and App version may be collected to diagnose crashes and improve App stability. This information cannot be used to identify you personally.

**Subscription Information:** If you purchase a subscription, Apple processes the transaction through its In-App Purchase system. We receive a subscription status (active, expired, etc.) but do not receive your payment details, credit card information, or Apple ID. Apple's privacy policy governs this transaction.

**Anonymous Analytics:** We may use anonymous analytics services (such as Apple's built-in analytics or RevenueCat for subscription analytics) to understand how the App is used in aggregate. This data is anonymized, does not include the contents of your journal entries, and cannot be tied back to you personally. You can opt out of Apple's analytics in your device settings.

## 3. iCloud Sync (If Enabled)

If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your journal entries are stored in your personal iCloud account, which is end-to-end encrypted by Apple in most cases. We do not have access to your iCloud data. Apple's privacy policy governs iCloud storage. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the App's settings.

## 4. Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind you of past entries. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device. The content of notifications is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can address it.

## 6. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

- **Apple App Store and In-App Purchases:** Required for distribution and subscriptions. Governed by Apple's privacy policy.
- **RevenueCat (or similar subscription manager):** Used to manage subscription state. Receives anonymized subscription data only.

We do not sell your information to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers. The App contains no third-party advertising.

## 7. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store the contents of your journal entries, there is no personal content for us to access, modify, or delete on our end.

You can:

- Delete the App at any time, which removes all locally stored data on your device
- Cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings
- Export your journal entries from within the App at any time
- Contact us with privacy questions at [YOUR EMAIL]

For users in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, California, or other jurisdictions with specific data protection rights (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may have additional rights including the right to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you. Because we do not hold your personal content, most of these requests are satisfied by deleting the App. For other requests, contact us at [YOUR EMAIL].

## 8. Data Retention

We do not retain the contents of your journal entries because we do not collect them. Any anonymous analytics data is retained only as long as necessary to maintain App functionality.

If you cancel your subscription, your journal entries on your device are not affected.

## 9. Security

We rely on the security features built into iOS and your device. Your journal entries are stored in the App's private container, which is sandboxed by iOS and inaccessible to other apps. We recommend protecting your device with a passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

## 10. AI and Machine Learning

We do not use the contents of your journal entries to train any AI or machine learning models. We do not provide your content to third-party AI services.
